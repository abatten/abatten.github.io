I'm a first year graduate student in the Centre of Astrophysics and Supercomputing, working with Associate Professor Alan Duffy and Associate Professor Emma Ryan-Weber.

My research involves looking at the metallicity of early galaxies and the intergalactic medium during the Epoch of Reionisation. To do this I am utilising a suite of cosmological radiative-hydrodynamic simulations called Aurora, and observational data from the MUSE instrument on the VLT.

My project is part of the Australian Research Council Centre of Excellent for All Sky Astrophysics in 3D (ASTRO 3D).
